Instructions

  • Click on the AC button before beginning each new calculation.
  • For square root of a number, first press the number then press the square root button.
  • An operation that results in a number too large to fit on the screen will display zero instead.
  • If a number has too many digits after the decimal point to fit on screen, some of the digits will be truncated.
  • Some operations like divide by zero, and square root of negative integers are not permitted.
